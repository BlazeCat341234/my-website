About

In late September, the idea to start a device repair business took shape. After much thought, we chose the name The Retro Guys — a simple promise to make repairs easy and reliable.

I'm Herbie Batsford, responsible for building and maintaining our website. But the heart of our business is a shared commitment between my dad, Gavin Batsford, and me. Together, we dedicate ourselves to delivering honest, high-quality device repairs with integrity.

As a father-and-son team, we understand the value of trust and transparency. We ensure our customers have peace of mind knowing they'll only be charged what's fair — enough to cover costs and a reasonable profit. If, despite our best precautions, we accidentally break something during a repair, you won't pay for that item and we will offer a replacement. We stand behind our work because your satisfaction and trust are our top priorities.
